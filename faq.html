    FAQ'S

    Does the Startup Builder Space have a minimum or maximum team size limit?

    You can have teams of upto 5 individuals.

    What kind of ideas does the Startup Builder Space support?

    All software based ideas and products are welcome.

    Are partnerships (groups of different developers/project partners) eligible for Startup Builder Space?

    Yes

    Can developers submit multiple ideas?

    At one time, we accept one idea/project/startup per team. Individuals who are part of one team cannot participate on another idea in the same cohort.

    What is the average duration of an Startup Builder Hub?

    Our weekend builder is a 48 hour event.
